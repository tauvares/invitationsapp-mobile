Our History

Started in 2016, Invitations App looks forward to be the most successful application related to events management, with features related not only to the invitations system, how to organize the events, plan all the tasks involved to each event separatedly and estimate the the costs for any specific event based on the quantities of guests.

Moreover, with continued use, the users can establish an average line of costs and plans based on same types of events

This web site has helped me to improve the quality of services I often provide to my customers, enabling me to put all details of my events in one single place.

João Tavares, IT technician at MPDFT, Brazil
